Stardew

Description

Farming Sim

Grow crops, tend to your animals, cultivate the land! With over 100+ items, animals, crops and buildings, you too can create your new peaceful life away from corporate greed.

Role Playing

Progress through the instricate story line of Stardew Valley by helping the nearby town. Complete quests to unlock cutscenes and to progress through the story line.

Exploration

Explore the vast lands of Stardew Valley. Traverse the dangerous deep caverns. Stardew offers multiple areas of exploration waiting for you to discover them.

Modding Support

With mod managers such as Thunder store, all users can import and create modpacks to spice up your gameplay. Choose from thousands of community made mods to make your gameplay different from the usual.
